e-Healthy Kids School Project (e-HKSP)

Integrated School Nutrition & Healthcare System

Overview

An integrated electronic school nutrition and preventive healthcare system designed to improve children's well-being by combining healthy meals, digital monitoring, and consultation services.

Project Idea

The project provides daily healthy meals, digital health profiles, screenings, and awareness programs through a unified digital platform connecting parents, schools, and specialists.

Project Products

Healthy Meal Program

Balanced meals for students

Health Profile

Digital student health records

Health Screening

Periodic health checks

Consultation

Online nutrition advice

Awareness Programs

Educational workshops

SWOT Analysis

Strengths: Integrated system, preventive healthcare

Weaknesses: High cost, complexity

Opportunities: Growing health awareness

Threats: Competition, regulations

Market Analysis

Target customers include students, parents, and schools. The project operates in nutrition, healthcare, and digital services markets.
