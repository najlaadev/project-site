e-Healthy Kids School Project

Smart School Nutrition & Healthcare System

Overview

The e-HKSP project is an integrated digital system designed to improve children’s health through balanced meals, health monitoring, and consultation services within schools.

Vision

To become a leading digital model in school health and nutrition.

Mission

Providing smart nutrition and healthcare solutions for students.

Project Goals

  • Improve students health
  • Reduce unhealthy food
  • Enable monitoring systems
  • Support digital transformation
