e-Healthy Kids School Project (e-HKSP)

Smart School Nutrition & Healthcare System

Vision

To become a leading digital model in school health and nutrition by enhancing children’s well-being through smart and sustainable solutions.

Mission

To provide integrated, technology-driven nutrition and preventive healthcare services for students through healthy meals, monitoring, and consultation.

Goals

  • Improve students’ health and nutrition habits
  • Reduce unhealthy food consumption
  • Provide continuous health monitoring
  • Enhance parent communication
  • Support digital transformation

Project Idea

The project aims to create a comprehensive digital system that combines healthy meal services, health monitoring, and awareness programs inside schools. It helps parents track their children’s health and ensures students follow a healthy lifestyle.

Our Services

1. Healthy Meal Program

Daily balanced meals designed for children based on nutritional standards.

2. Digital Health Profile

Each student has a digital record including BMI and health data.

3. Health Screening

Periodic health checks inside schools to detect risks early.

4. Nutrition Consultation

Online consultation services for parents with specialists.

5. Awareness Programs

Workshops to educate students about healthy eating habits.
